The DIY Project Tracker

For keeping your home improvement project on time and on budget.

The Scenario

Meet Michael from Mandeville. He's decided to finally build the backyard deck he's been dreaming of. He set a budget based on some initial research from Rapid True Value and Hardware & Lumber, and he has a rough idea of the steps involved.

But as the project starts, unexpected costs pop upâ€”the price of cement went up, he needed more screws than he thought. He's losing track of his spending and the timeline is starting to stretch. What started as an exciting project is becoming a source of stress.

The Headache:

"Will this project ever get finished, and how much will it *really* cost me?"

The Solution: A Blueprint for Success

This tracker is Michael's project foreman. It allows him to break down the entire project into manageable tasks and assign a budget to each one. As he buys materials and completes steps, he can log expenses and check off tasks, giving him a real-time overview of his budget and progress.

The "Budget vs. Actual" KPI is a game-changer, instantly showing him if he's overspending in any area. This allows him to make adjustments on the fly, preventing small overages from derailing the entire project. It brings order to the chaos of a DIY project.
