DIY & Projects Tracker

Keep your renovation or personal project on time and on budget.

The Scenario

"Maria is helping her brother renovate his apartment in Spanish Town. She's in charge of the budget, buying materials, and paying the workmen. With receipts, invoices, and cash payments all over the place, she's constantly worried about going over budget and forgetting an important task. She needs one central place to see everything."

The Solution

The DIY & Projects Tracker is a simple command center for Maria's renovation project. It allows her to log every expense against the total budget, giving her a real-time view of how much money is left. The integrated to-do list helps her track tasks, from buying tiles to paying the electrician, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks and the project stays on track financially.
