The Freelancer & WFH Dashboard

For turning your side hustle into a serious, organized business.

The Scenario

Meet Simone from Spanish Town. She's a talented graphic designer whose freelance work is booming. She has several clients, from a small bakery in Kingston to a guesthouse on the North Coast. She's busy, which is great, but her admin is a mess.

She's tracking payments in a notebook, project deadlines on sticky notes, and expenses are just a pile of receipts. She isn't sure which clients are late on payments or how much profit she *actually* made last month after paying for her software subscriptions and other costs.

The Headache:

"Am I running a business, or just a chaotic hobby?"

The Solution: Your Business Command Centre

This dashboard is designed to be Simone's virtual business manager. It brings all her critical information into one clean, easy-to-understand view. She can see her monthly revenue at a glance, track which invoices are paid and which are overdue, and monitor the progress of all her active projects.

Most importantly, the "Monthly Profit" KPI gives her the true financial health of her business. This clarity empowers her to make smarter decisions, price her services confidently, and focus on what she does best: creative design work.
