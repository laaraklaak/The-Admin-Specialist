Freelancer & WFH Dashboard

Manage your side hustle or small business with confidence.

The Scenario

"As a freelance Admin Specialist, Laura juggles multiple clients. She needs a simple way to track her income from different projects, manage her business expenses for tax time, and see the overall financial health of her business at a glance. A complex accounting software is too much, but a simple notebook isn't enough."

The Solution

This dashboard is the perfect middle ground. It's a simple, powerful tool that gives Laura a clear, real-time view of her business finances. She can easily log income and expenses, see her monthly profit, and track which clients have paid. It provides the professional organization she needs to make smart business decisions and be prepared when it's time to file her taxes.
