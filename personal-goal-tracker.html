The Personal Goal Tracker

For when you have a big dream and need a clear path to get there.

The Scenario

Meet Anna from Kingston. She's a dedicated nurse with a dream of buying her first car. She's been trying to save, but life happens. Between helping her family, monthly bills, and the occasional treat, her savings account isn't growing as fast as she'd hoped.

She feels a little lost, unsure if she's on track or just spinning her wheels. The goal feels so far away it's hard to stay motivated.

The Headache:

"Am I actually making progress toward my goal?"

The Solution: A Clear View of the Finish Line

This tracker was designed for Anna. It's not just about numbers; it's about clarity and motivation. By setting a clear goal and target date, she can see exactly how much she needs to save each month. Every deposit feels like a victory because she can instantly see the progress bar move and the "months left" number go down.

It transforms a vague dream into a concrete, achievable plan, helping her make smarter spending decisions because the reward is right there on the screen.
