Personal Goal Tracker

Turn your big dreams into an achievable, step-by-step plan.

The Scenario

"Anna, a young professional in Kingston, dreams of buying her first car. She saves what she can each month, but without a clear plan, the goal feels distant and sometimes impossible. She isn't sure if she's on track, how far she has to go, or when she might actually reach her goal. This uncertainty is discouraging."

The Solution

The Personal Goal Tracker is a simple, visual dashboard that transforms Anna's dream into a tangible project. It gives her a single place to see her progress in real-time, providing the motivation and clarity she needs to stay focused. Instead of guessing, she knows exactly where she stands, making her feel empowered and in control of her financial future.
