The Spending & Savings Tracker

For when you need to know exactly where your money is going, every month.

The Scenario

Meet David from Portmore. He's a young professional with a steady job, but at the end of the month, he's always surprised by how little is left in his account. He wants to start saving seriously for a down payment and pay down a small car loan faster.

He tries to keep track in his head, but the small purchases—a patty here, a box lunch there, a top-up for his phone—add up faster than he realizes. He feels like he's working hard but not getting ahead.

The Headache:

"Where did all my money go this month?"

The Solution: A Simple, Honest Look at Your Habits

This dashboard gives David the clarity he's missing. Instead of guessing, he can see a simple, visual breakdown of his spending. The pie chart instantly shows him that "Food & Dining" is his biggest expense category, which is the insight he needs to make a change.

By logging transactions as they happen, he can finally create a realistic budget, identify areas to cut back, and confidently allocate more money toward his savings and debt repayment goals. It's about taking control, not feeling restricted.
