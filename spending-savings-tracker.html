Meet David: The Careful Planner

David from Portmore is careful with his money, but at the end of the month, he's always surprised by how little is left. He doesn't have big loans, but wants to save more effectively. He needs a simple tool to see exactly where his money is going so he can find opportunities to save.

The Solution: A Clear View of Your Spending

The Spending & Savings Tracker is designed for David. It removes the complexity of debt tracking and focuses on two things: monitoring spending and accelerating savings goals, providing a clear path to building a stronger financial future.

How It Works: A Simple Process

The power of the dashboard lies in its simple, automated workflow. A consistent habit of logging transactions is all it takes to maintain a clear, real-time view of your entire financial picture.
